Here is some helpful info to get you started:

Chrome doesn't pin new extensions to your toolbar for you. Here's how to do that for easier access:
1. Click in the toolbar.
2. Click next to PresetNinja.
How to use:
1. Hover your cursor over images to check if they contain the necessary metadata to generate a preset -- not all images will work. Visit our documentation to learn more.
2. Right click the image to download your preset.
3. Import your preset(s) into Lightroom or Camera Raw. We will have detailed instructions posted soon, but in the mean time here some helpful links.
Lightroom Lightroom Classic Adobe Camera Raw
Sometimes you'll find that hovering over an image does not seem to do anything. We have some helpful info on how to get around this.
